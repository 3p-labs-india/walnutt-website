## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide

**Candidates:**
- Full name and email address
- Target role and experience level
- Assessment responses and answers
- GitHub profile URL and linked portfolio content
- Resume or work history (if uploaded)
- Any additional information you choose to share on your Walnutt profile

- Contact person name and designation

- **AI mentor interaction logs** — records of guidance and recommendations provided by our AI mentor during the learning phase
- **Candidate-company match scores** — AI-generated compatibility scores between candidate profiles and job descriptions

### 1.3 Information Collected Automatically

- Device information (browser type, operating system, device type)
- IP address and approximate location (city/region level)
- Usage data (pages visited, time spent, features used, assessment completion patterns)
- Cookies and similar tracking technologies (see Section 7)

## 3. How Our AI Works With Your Data

Walnutt uses artificial intelligence and machine learning across several core functions of the Platform. We believe in being transparent about how AI interacts with your data.

**AI-Powered Assessments:** When you take an assessment, our AI engine adapts questions in real time based on your responses. It evaluates your answers to generate scores across multiple engineering dimensions. The AI analyses your problem-solving approach, technical reasoning, and decision-making — not just whether an answer is correct.

**AI Mentor:** During the learning and skill-building phase, our AI mentor provides personalised guidance, suggests resources, and recommends projects based on your assessment results and progress. The AI processes your interaction history to tailor its recommendations.

**AI Interviews:** For candidates matched with companies, our AI conducts structured technical interviews calibrated to the role requirements. The AI generates evaluation scorecards based on the interview responses.

**AI Matching:** Our AI processes candidate skill profiles and company job descriptions to generate compatibility match scores.

**Important notes about our AI:**
- AI-generated scores and evaluations are based on your responses and publicly available information you have linked (such as GitHub profiles). We do not use data from external sources you have not provided or linked.
- Our AI models may be updated and improved over time. When we make significant changes to how AI processes your data, we will update this policy.
- You may request a human review of any AI-generated assessment or evaluation by contacting us at privacy@walnutt.com.

## 4. How We Share Your Information

**We do not sell your personal information.**

We may share your information in the following circumstances:

**With Companies (for Candidates):** When you opt to make your profile visible, companies on the Platform can view your Walnutt skill profile, including your readiness score, dimension breakdowns, and linked portfolio. Companies cannot see your raw assessment responses — only the evaluated results.

**With Candidates (for Companies):** Your company name, role descriptions, and hiring preferences may be visible to matched candidates.

**Service Providers:** We may share information with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating the Platform (hosting, analytics, email delivery). These providers are contractually bound to use your data only for the purposes we specify.

**Legal Requirements:** We may disclose information if required by law, regulation, legal process, or enforceable government request.

**Business Transfers:** In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you of any such change.

## 6. Data Retention

- **Account data** (name, email) is retained for as long as your account is active.
- **Assessment data** (responses, scores, AI evaluations) is retained for as long as your account is active and for up to 12 months after account deletion, to allow for any pending processes or disputes.
- **AI interview data** is retained for 12 months after the interview date.
- **Usage and analytics data** is retained in anonymised form and may be kept indefinitely for platform improvement.
- You may request deletion of your data at any time (see Section 8).

## 8. Your Rights

Under applicable Indian law, including the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDPA), you have the following rights:

- **Right to Access:** Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
- **Right to Correction:** Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
- **Right to Erasure:** Request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal retention requirements.
- **Right to Withdraw Consent:** Withdraw your consent for data processing at any time. This will not affect the lawfulness of processing carried out before withdrawal.
- **Right to Grievance Redressal:** Raise a complaint about our data practices.
- **Right to Nominate:** Nominate another individual to exercise your rights in the event of your death or incapacity.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at **privacy@walnutt.com**.

We will respond to your request within 30 days.
